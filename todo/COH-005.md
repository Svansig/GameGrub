## Problem

Launch and auth logic boundaries are inconsistent, which increases coupling and makes ownership unclear.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Define use-case entry points for launch/auth flows.
  - Move one representative flow behind the use-case boundary.
- Out of scope:
  - Full service rewrite.
