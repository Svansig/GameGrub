## Problem

State and one-off effects are represented inconsistently across ViewModels.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Define standard patterns for state, events, and effects.
  - Apply standards to at least two high-traffic ViewModels.
- Out of scope:
  - Migration of every ViewModel.

## Analysis

- `BaseViewModel<S, E>` exists with state/effect pattern:
  - `MutableStateFlow` for state with `asStateFlow()` exposure
  - `Channel` for effects with `receiveAsFlow()` exposure
  - `setState()`, `updateState()`, `sendEffect()` helpers
- `BaseScreenViewModel` extends with loading/success/error/error helpers
- **Current state**: No ViewModels actually extend BaseViewModel - they all directly extend ViewModel
- This represents an opportunity for consistency but also a risk (breaking change)
