## Problem

Device and hardware reads were scattered across multiple layers (`Build`, `Settings`, `GPUInformation`, `/proc`, `/sys`), which increased duplication and made behavior harder to test.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Introduce a central query boundary and Android-backed manager.
  - Route key service/UI/util call sites through the boundary.
  - Remove legacy direct-query utility wrappers targeted for retirement.
- Out of scope:
  - Full test-suite expansion for all hardware edge-cases.
