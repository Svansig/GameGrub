## Problem

Legacy utility access patterns (`StorageUtils`, `FileUtils`, `MarkerUtils`) bypass a central storage boundary and make policy enforcement difficult.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Replace utility call paths with storage gateways/managers.
  - Add temporary compatibility shims where needed, then remove them.
  - Enforce “no direct storage operations outside storage package” rule for migrated areas.
- Out of scope:
  - Non-storage utility cleanup.
