# COH-028 - Move manifest and update flows out of utils

## Problem

Manifest repository/installer/component availability logic and app update check/install logic are feature owners currently placed under `utils`.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Move `ManifestModels`, `ManifestRepository`, `ManifestInstaller`, and `ManifestComponentHelper` under explicit component/content ownership package(s).
  - Move `UpdateChecker` and `UpdateInstaller` under app update feature package.
  - Remove coupling to `utils.manifest` and `utils.network` call paths.
- Out of scope:
  - New manifest schema behavior changes.
