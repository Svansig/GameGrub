## Problem

External URLs are duplicated across UI and settings flows (notably Discord and remote manifests), making updates error-prone and obscuring ownership boundaries.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Centralize duplicated external links/endpoints into a shared constants owner.
  - Replace duplicated literals in audited call sites.
  - Keep endpoint behavior unchanged.
- Out of scope:
  - Introducing new endpoints or environment behavior changes.
