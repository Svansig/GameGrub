## Problem

`GameGrubApp.onCreate` directly orchestrates many startup concerns (network, crash handling, preferences, launch cleanup, analytics, integrity warmup), which increases coupling and makes startup behavior harder to test.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Introduce explicit startup initializer owners (for example, a startup coordinator and focused initializer classes).
  - Move direct static initializer calls behind injected boundaries where feasible.
  - Remove hidden startup coupling (for example, injected dependencies used only for side effects) or document and isolate it.
- Out of scope:
  - Changing product behavior for startup flows beyond ownership and testability improvements.
