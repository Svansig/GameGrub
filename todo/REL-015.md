## Problem

`OrientationManager` currently has lifecycle and correctness risks (non-idempotent start, dead logic branches, and weak handling around sensor edge inputs), which can lead to stale or inconsistent orientation behavior.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Make `startOrientator()` and `stopOrientator()` idempotent.
  - Remove dead/unreachable orientation-selection logic and simplify decision flow.
  - Harden `ORIENTATION_UNKNOWN` and boundary-angle handling.
  - Reduce redundant `requestedOrientation` writes.
- Out of scope:
  - New user-facing orientation features.
  - Expanding orientation controls beyond existing product requirements.
