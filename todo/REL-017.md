## Problem

Inline delay/retry/timeout literals are spread across critical flows (connection, prelaunch retries, focus restoration), reducing readability and making policy tuning risky.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Replace inline timing/retry literals with named constants near their owners.
  - Use unit-suffixed constant names (`*_MS`, `*_SECONDS`, `*_RETRIES`).
  - Preserve runtime behavior and sequence semantics.
- Out of scope:
  - Changing retry strategies or timeout durations.
