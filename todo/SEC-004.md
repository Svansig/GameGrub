# SEC-004 - Minimize sensitive data exposure in logs

- **ID**: `SEC-004`
- **Area**: `logging`
- **Priority**: `P2`
- **Status**: `Backlog`
- **Owner**: `TBD`

## Problem

Logs may expose sensitive values when debugging auth/network flows.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Audit logs for tokens, identifiers, and sensitive payload fragments.
  - Introduce redaction helpers where needed.
- Out of scope:
  - Removing all diagnostic logging.
