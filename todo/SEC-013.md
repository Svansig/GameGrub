## Problem

Debug artifacts may persist more sensitive fields than necessary.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Inventory sensitive fields in debug outputs/storage.
  - Reduce or redact where practical.
- Out of scope:
  - Eliminating all debug artifacts.
