# SRV-009 - Remove remaining class fields that belong in domains

- **ID**: `SRV-009`
- **Area**: `service/steam`
- **Priority**: `P1`
- **Status**: `Backlog`
- **Owner**: `TBD`

## Problem

SteamService class body has fields that should be in domains:
- `picsGetProductInfoJob`, `picsChangesCheckerJob` - belong in SteamPicsSyncDomain (already injected)
- `friendCheckerJob` - belongs in SteamAccountDomain (friends manager)
- `retryAttempt` - connection policy, could stay in service or move to connection domain
- `reconnectJob` - connection management, could stay

The domain injection is already there (picsSyncDomain, accountDomain), but the job fields are still in service. The domains should own their own job lifecycle.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Move PICS job fields to picsSyncDomain
  - Move friendCheckerJob to accountDomain/friendsManager
  - Remove duplicate job tracking from service
- Out of scope:
  - Changes to how jobs are started/canceled

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] PICS jobs tracked in domain, not service
- [ ] Friend checker job tracked in domain, not service
