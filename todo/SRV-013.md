# SRV-013 - Consolidate shutdown/cleanup helpers

- **ID**: `SRV-013`
- **Area**: `service/steam`
- **Priority**: `P2`
- **Status**: `Backlog`
- **Owner**: `TBD`

## Problem

Shutdown and cleanup helper methods in SteamService companion:
- `clearUserData(...)` - belongs in accountDomain or libraryDomain
- `shouldClearUserDataForLoggedOnFailure(...)` - belongs in accountDomain
- `clearDatabase(...)` - belongs in libraryDomain
- `cancelLongLivedSteamJobs()` - belongs in respective domains
- `performLogOffDuties()` - belongs in accountDomain

These should be moved to appropriate domains.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Move cleanup methods to domains
  - Keep simple state setters in service
- Out of scope:
  - Changes to what data is cleared

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Cleanup methods moved to domains
