## Problem

Companion wrappers (`getAppInfoOf`, `getPkgInfoOf`, etc.) keep callers tied to global service state.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Define caller-facing gateway for app/license/download lookup.
  - Migrate selected callers from companion wrappers to injected gateway.
- Out of scope:
  - Deleting all wrappers in one ticket.
