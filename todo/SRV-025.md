## Problem

Non-Steam platform services expose wide companion APIs that promote global access patterns.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Inventory companion API entry points in non-Steam services.
  - Reduce or deprecate selected global-style entry points.
- Out of scope:
  - Full cross-platform service redesign.
