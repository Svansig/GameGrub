## Problem

`SteamNotifications` is available in JavaSteam but currently not wired in GameNative. We need evidence on whether it improves account/session reliability or user experience for Android gameplay workflows.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Inventory `SteamNotifications` callback/event surface relevant to session/account state.
  - Identify realistic Android UX touchpoints (foreground notifications, in-app banners, retry hints).
  - Estimate implementation complexity, risk, and test strategy.
  - Produce a `Go / Defer / Drop` decision with rationale.
- Out of scope:
  - Full production implementation.

## Acceptance Criteria
