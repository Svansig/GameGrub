## Problem

Some Steam titles rely on Game Coordinator flows. GameNative currently does not wire `SteamGameCoordinator`, and it is unclear whether support would materially improve real-world title compatibility.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Identify game categories/titles likely to benefit from coordinator support.
  - Assess protocol complexity, maintenance risk, and anti-cheat constraints.
  - Determine whether feature should be generalized or title-gated.
  - Produce a `Go / Defer / Drop` decision with rationale.
- Out of scope:
  - Broad multiplayer feature implementation.
