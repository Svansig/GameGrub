## Problem

Direct event bus subscriptions in composables increase lifecycle risk.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Move selected subscriptions to ViewModel/coordinator layer.
  - Keep composables reactive to state/effects.
- Out of scope:
  - Event system replacement.
