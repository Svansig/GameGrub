## Problem

Orientation sensing currently depends on one-shot UI-driven startup events, which can leave orientation handling disabled after lifecycle transitions.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Make `MainActivity` the lifecycle owner for orientation sensing enable/disable.
  - Remove reliance on one-time startup timing for orientator activation.
  - Ensure behavior remains stable with `configChanges` orientation handling.
- Out of scope:
  - Reworking unrelated lifecycle responsibilities.
  - Route-specific orientation policy changes (handled by child ticket).
